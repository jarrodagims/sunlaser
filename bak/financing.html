Financing

Vision Correction in an investment in your quality of life. We offer various financing options through different banks and lending companies. You may of course also obtain your own financing. Please discuss with our surgical coordinator any current updates as to rates or current lenders we are working with to help make LASIK affordable to everyone.

0% Financing available

Current options include traditional financing with monthly payments as low as $97 or 0%-financing options over 6 to 24 month terms. Our counselors can provide complete financing information during your FREE LASIK consultation and help you select the finance plan that best meets your needs.
