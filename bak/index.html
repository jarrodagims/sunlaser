The pace of technological development and disease understanding in medicine continues to increase bringing more ophthalmology treatment options for patients than ever before. Sun Laser Vision Center is committed to bringing our patients and the southwest community the latest advances in vision correction surgery and treatments for complex eye conditions. We are leading the way in adopting new technology in laser refractive surgery, advanced options in cataract surgery and the latest management of keratoconus.

El Paso’s leader in surgical vision correction

LASIK

From wavefront guided and wavefront optimized treatments to topography driven treatment and iLASIK, Sun Laser Vision Center is the only surgical center in El Paso to use two laser platforms. Offering the greatest number of options in laser technology allows for better customization for our patients.

Laser Cataract Surgery

Great news for cataract patients, laser cataract surgery has arrived. Using the latest advances in femtosecond laser techniques and modern technology lens implants we can provide better, more consistent vision outcomes with lower dependency on glasses than ever before.

Corneal Cross Linking and Intacs

Keratoconus and corneal ectasia can be debilitating conditions leading to poor vision or vision loss at times uncorrectable even by glasses or contacts. Corneal cross linking and Intacs are two new developments that are helping to improve vision quality and stop the progression of this disease.

General Eye Care

Despite the continued gains in technology and disease understanding, it’s important to recognize when patients cannot be helped with surgery. Whether it’s optimizing vision with glasses or complex fit contacts, we’re ready to help.
